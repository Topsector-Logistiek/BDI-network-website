Toolkit

The Topsector Logistiek supports early BDI adoptors by providing a toolkit with ready to consume building blocks for development and testing. Currently one building block, the iSHARE authorisation register is available for production deployments. Currently two building blocks are available as a service:

  • Triple store – TriplyDB is available as a service for development and test
  • iSHARE authorisation register – the Poort8 authorisation register is available as a service for development, test and production.

To gain access to the building blocks, please contact the Adoption Support Team (AST).
